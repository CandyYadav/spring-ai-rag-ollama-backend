Section: Travel Booking Policy

Employees must book travel tickets at least 7 days in advance.

All bookings (flight/train) should be made through the company-approved travel portal.

Last-minute bookings require manager approval.


Section: Accommodation Policy

Employees are eligible for hotel stays up to ₹4,000 per night.

Only standard business hotels are allowed.

Luxury hotels are not permitted unless approved by senior management.


Section: Daily Allowance Policy

Employees will receive a daily allowance of ₹1,000 for food and local travel.

Bills are not required for daily allowance claims.

Allowance is applicable only for official travel days.


Section: Local Transport Policy

Employees can use cabs or public transport for official work.

Ride-sharing services like Ola and Uber are allowed.

Personal vehicle usage will be reimbursed at ₹10 per km.


Section: Travel Reimbursement Policy

All travel expenses must be submitted within 10 days after trip completion.

Original bills are required for reimbursement (except daily allowance).

Reimbursement will be processed within 7 working days.
